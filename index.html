About Me

My name is Khaled Essam. I am a Data Analyst focused on SQL Server, Data Warehousing, ETL development, data modeling, and business intelligence reporting. I build end-to-end data projects using Medallion Architecture, starting from raw source data and ending with clean, analytics-ready models.
